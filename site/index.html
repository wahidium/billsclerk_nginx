Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

From invoice to accountant-ready bundle — without manual entry, without new accounting software.

Any invoice, any format

Upload PDFs, forward invoice emails to your dedicated address, or snap a photo of a paper receipt. BillsClerk handles vendor PDFs, scanned documents, and phone photos equally well — if it's an invoice, we'll read it.

Expand
Review side-by-side

View the original invoice document alongside its extracted entries at any time. Edit any field — vendor, category, GL code, amount — and confirm when you're happy. Nothing changes until you say so.

Expand
Accountant's Bundle export

Filter entries by month, quarter, or any date range and export a ZIP file containing your bookkeeping entries in Excel plus every underlying source invoice. One file. Hand it over. Done.

Expand
Bank & card reconciliation

Upload bank or credit card statements (CSV, Excel, or PDF) and BillsClerk matches them against your entries. Unmatched items are flagged clearly — so you arrive at your accountant meeting with gaps already identified.

Expand
Smart GL & heading suggestions

AI suggests accounting headings and GL codes drawn from your own chart of accounts — with sensible defaults if you haven't set one up yet. Gets smarter with every correction you make.

Tags & custom grouping

Create tags and apply them to any entry, then filter and export separately by tag. Perfect for property owners tracking costs per rental, or any business needing per-project or per-client breakdowns.
